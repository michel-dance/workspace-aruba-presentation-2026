Talos Train Automation · The Why

Railroad Pain Points

  • Safety—hazardous materials demand predictable handling.
  • Fuel consumption—the dominant operating cost.
  • Aging operator fleet—talent pipeline is shrinking.
  • Maintenance—large fleets need proactive care.

Our Solution

Caterpillar is building automated solutions across construction, manufacturing, mining, and rail.

Heavy investment in digital transformation and an AI-first culture accelerates how we deliver.

Talos delivers automated handling that matches or exceeds expert engineers while improving fuel efficiency.

Field Deployment · BNSF Snapshot

BNSF runs Talos-equipped locomotives across one of the largest rail footprints in North America.
